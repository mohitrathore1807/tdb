Tech Driven Basic (TDB) is a computer vision company with a vision of providing AI softwares in various domains such as Medical, Real estate, Government, Retails, Construction. We provide our clients with various camera enabling tools as an autonomous tool, life saviour tool, decision making software, Edge surveillance, IOT.
With having a strong vision and experience, Tech Driven Basic Provides solutions for the real time problems using computer vision techniques and artificial intelligence which reduces the need of manual monitoring.
Our vision is to provide affordable AI and reduce human level monitoring.

Timeline

The Journey Of TDB.ai. Let's roll the Camera

1 / 8
The Begining

We say every success story starts with a dream, Our Director Mr. Deepanshu Bhinda with an idea and aim of providing affordable and accurate AI softwares to people of various domains started a company TDB Technologies on 15th March,2019 as a sole proprietorship firm.

2 / 8
The Hardwork

We got the first opportunity to introduce ourselves to youth and public soon after the starting of firm on 1st April, 2019 where we were asked to provide a workshop on AI at SKNCOE Pune.

3 / 8
The Adaptation

On 20th April, 2019 we got our very first project from a singapore client base on Automatic Number Plate Recognition(ANPR) and then we got an opportunity to build a complete product on road safety by a UAE based client.

4 / 8
The Consistency

We started conducting workshops on AI in various colleges and universities like Symbiosis University of Applied Science Indore, Swami Vivekanand College of Engineering and many more..

5 / 8
The Conversion

While Covid-19 pandemic brought many things and many lives to a standstill it also gave opportunities. We were lucky to have got many opportunities during this time and got to work on COVID-19 precautionary like temperature detection, heart rate variation, mask detection and many more.

6 / 8
The Incorporation

On July 28th, 2020 almost after a year of starting the firm, we got blessed to have incorporated Tech Driven Basic AI into a Private Limited Company and we know this is just the beginning.

7 / 8
The New Begining

On 25th november 2020 we have collaborated with one of the best IT networking solution based company in hydrabad named veloxtech solutions in order to integrate AI with existing software in industry.

8 / 8
The Consulting

On December 16th 2020, We and Veloxtech Solutions have started an AI consulting firm SolvingScale.ai which focuses on solving problems with AI.
